\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a \doxylink{class_banker}{Banker} who makes lowball offers. 

The \doxylink{class_greedy_banker}{Greedy\+Banker} aims to maximize the bank\textquotesingle{}s profit by making offers significantly lower than the average remaining amounts.
