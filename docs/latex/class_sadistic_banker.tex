\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a \doxylink{class_banker}{Banker} who makes the worst possible offers. 

The \doxylink{class_sadistic_banker}{Sadistic\+Banker} focuses on frustrating the player by making offers that are significantly lower than expected.
